Frequently asked questions

How is this different from coding copilots?

Coding copilots implement what you ask for. SleepMode PM tells you what to build by analyzing market trends, competitor moves, and lead generation opportunities—then implements the approved choice.

Do I need to give production credentials?

No. For onboarding, SleepMode PM runs changes in isolated Daytona sandboxes with preview URLs and controlled branch workflows.

Can I approve from mobile?

Yes. Decision cards can be approved from Telegram with one tap.

How does SleepMode PM track market trends and competitor features?

SleepMode PM uses web scraping via Oxylabs to monitor competitor websites, product launches, and feature updates in real-time. It analyzes industry trends, user demand patterns, and emerging technologies to identify opportunities before they become mainstream.

What kind of lead generation insights does it provide?

The system identifies features that drive customer acquisition, analyzes conversion bottlenecks, tracks what attracts users to competitor products, and recommends changes that improve signup rates and user retention based on market data.

How long does it take to set up and start getting insights?

Setup takes about 5 minutes. Connect your repository, add competitor URLs to monitor, and the system starts analyzing immediately. You'll receive your first decision cards within 24 hours as the AI completes its initial market analysis and codebase scan.

Who is SleepMode PM best suited for?

Solo founders who need product direction, small development teams without a dedicated PM, startups competing in fast-moving markets, and product teams who want data-driven prioritization instead of gut-feel roadmaps.

What integrations and tools does SleepMode PM support?

Integrates with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket for repository access. Uses Daytona for sandbox environments. Sends notifications via Telegram, Slack, and email. Supports CI/CD workflows and provides API access for custom integrations on Growth and Enterprise plans.

Is my code and business data secure?

Yes. All code analysis happens in isolated Daytona sandboxes. We use bank-level encryption for data at rest and in transit. Competitor monitoring uses public data only. Enterprise plans include SOC 2 compliance, private cloud deployment, and custom data retention policies.
